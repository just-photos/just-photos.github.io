About Me

I am an amateur photographer and have been practicing my hobby for over 45 years. I started with a Kodak Instamatic, fixed focus pocket camera handed to me by my Dad because he didn't want to take pics ... and I was hooked. I have used a variety of equipment over the years but my iPhone is go-to camera these days as it is always in my pocket.
